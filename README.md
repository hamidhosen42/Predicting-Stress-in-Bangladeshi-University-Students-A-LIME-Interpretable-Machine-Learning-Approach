### Predicting-Stress-in-Bangladeshi-University-Students-A-LIME-Interpretable-Machine-Learning-Approach

University students suffers from high levels of stress, which
adversely affects their academic performance, mental and physical health,
and overall well-being. In this research paper, we use machine learning
algorithms and explainable artificial intelligence (XAI) techniques to
predict stress levels among university students. The study collected data
from multiple universities in Bangladesh and used machine learning
algorithms to determine stress levels accurately. Support Vector Classifier
(SVC) performed well, with 90% accuracy. A Lime method was also used
to understand the model's behavior and address any biases. Stress levels
were identified by analyzing specific observations. These results provided
both transparency and understandability. We aim to develop prediction
models that can identify at-risk individuals from this research and reveal
the causes of stress among university students. It is possible to minimize
the negative effects on students' academic performance and well-being by
detecting stress levels early.
